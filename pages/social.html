About Social WellBeing

Social

Social well-being of an organisation refers to the overall health and satisfaction of its employees, as well as the impact the organisation has on the broader community and society. It encompasses several key dimensions such as employee satisfaction, diversity and inclusion, mental and physical health, community engagement, giving back to the society and have having an overall positive impact on a broader group of stakeholders such as suppliers, customers, investors and lenders.
